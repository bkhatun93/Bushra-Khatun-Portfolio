Final Report Summary

A. Were we successful?

Yes. The project was successful because the website progressed from a prototype into a polished final product with stronger content, better structure, and improved usability.

B. Milestones and subtasks

Major milestones included planning, prototype development, content expansion, usability improvement, and final testing. Subtasks included case study creation, navigation refinement, responsiveness updates, and final review.

C. Missed deadlines and management lessons

A few content-related tasks took longer than expected because content writing and design iteration required more time. In future projects, adding buffer time, breaking work into smaller deliverables, and reviewing progress weekly would reduce delays.

D. What we learned

The past 8 weeks reinforced the value of realistic planning, revision cycles, user experience design, and disciplined time management.

E. Future impact

Completing this project strengthened both technical and professional skills, especially in organizing work, presenting outcomes, and managing a project from idea to delivery.
